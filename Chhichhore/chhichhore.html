Chhichhore

A heartwarming tale of friendship, love, and life's ups and downs, directed by Nitesh Tiwari. A tragic incident forces Anirudh, a middle-aged man, to take a trip down memory lane and reminisce his college days along with his friends, who were labelled as losers.

Cast

Sushant Singh Rajput as Aniruddh "Anni" Pathak

Shraddha Kapoor as Maya

Varun Sharma as Sexa
